NutriFlow Site Plan

WDD 231 Individual Website Project

Site Name

NutriFlow

The name NutriFlow combines the ideas of nutrition and continuous progress. It represents a smooth and structured way to track daily eating habits and health goals. The name is short, modern, and easy to remember, making it suitable for a digital health tracking platform.

Site Purpose

NutriFlow is a personal nutrition tracking website designed to help users follow a structured weekly meal plan. The platform will display daily nutrition goals such as protein, carbohydrates, vegetables, and water intake. Users will be able to track their progress, filter by week, view detailed daily information through modal dialogs, and save their data using local storage. The website will also include a dashboard to summarize progress and a form to log daily intake.

Scenarios

  • What are my nutrition goals for today?
  • How much water, protein, and vegetables have I completed this week?
  • Can I save my daily progress and return later without losing my data?
  • Which week of the nutrition plan am I currently viewing?
